About

This site was created to replace the Serenity Games home page and to give members a way to connect with each other. It is meant to be easily maintainable, portable, and secure.

What about the forums?

The forums have been taken down due to lack of use. phpBB takes quite a bit of time to maintain and it is no longer worth the time or effort. tjbenator may take the time some day to generate a static version of the forums.

How do I update my page?

To update your page, contact tjbenator or create an issue/pull request on Github!
